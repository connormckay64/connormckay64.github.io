Inward Bound is one of Canberra's biggest sporting events. Backing Griffin's squad puts your brand in front of a massive community.

Why back us

Where your support goes

Running Inward Bound is far from free. Entry fees, transport to and from remote drop points, safety and navigation gear, and months of training all add up to a sizeable quantity.

Sponsorship covers the real costs and allows us to keep training and return to this unique event. In return, your brand gets known by the hundreds of runners and spectators on course and at Endpoint, and the thousands of spectators on the IB Live Website, the IB TV coverage, or ANU social media.
