Why back us

Where your support goes

Running Inward Bound isn't free. Entry fees, transport to and from remote drop points, safety and navigation gear, and months of training all add up — and Griffin's runners come from across Canberra rather than a subsidised residential budget.

Sponsorship covers the real costs and keeps the squad open to anyone, regardless of what they can pay. In return, your brand travels with a team that hundreds of students, alumni and spectators follow every year — on course, at Endpoint, and across the IB Live stream.
